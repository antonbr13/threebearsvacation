Sandpoint & More


Sandpoint has that quintessential small town vibe - complete with an active main street (shopping, art galleries, restuarants and a crystal/gem store), a sand beach on Lake Pend Oreille, pedestrian and bicycle paths in and around town and of course it is still only 30 mintues from Schweitzer Mountain Resort.
