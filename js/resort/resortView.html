Dover Bay Resort


Three Bears Bungalow and Three Bears Cottage are both situated within Dover Bay Resort, which is 3 miles from Sandpoint, Idaho.

Dover Bay Resort is a wonderful vacation destination for families and groups of all ages. Nestled along the shores of the mighty Pend Oreille River, while still only a 30 minute drive to Schweitzer Mountain Resort - Dover Bay is the perfect vacation experience for fun year round.

Resort Amenities

  • Pool & Hot Tub
  • Exercise & Weight Room
  • Boat Launch
  • Kids Playground
  • Tennis Court
  • Sand Volleyball
  • Dish Restaurant
  • Resort General Store
  • Bike and Pedestian Paths
