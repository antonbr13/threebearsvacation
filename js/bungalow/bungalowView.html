Bungalow


This cozy dwelling makes great use of space with a high faulted ceiling and a loft like upstairs that is secluded enough for a whole family and open enough for a fun get away for the young ones. The upstairs features a a queen bed, a bunk bed, a reading nook and a full restroom.

The main floor consists of a master bedroom with a queen bed and full bathroom. The second bedroom downstairs also has a queen bed and there is a third full restroom for the main level as well. The kitchen and living room are cozy and can be shared by the entire group. Don't forget the front porch patio complete with BBQ and bar seating.
